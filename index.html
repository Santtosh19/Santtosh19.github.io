My Engineering Philosophy

My focus lies at the intersection of data science, software automation, and hardware reliability. I transform raw manufacturing and test data into actionable intelligence that prevents defects and accelerates development.

From developing end-to-end failure analysis workflows and deploying predictive maintenance models, to designing robust CI/CD pipelines for mission-critical analytics tools, my work is centered on one principle: using technology to build more resilient and reliable products.

I thrive on tackling systemic challenges—proactively identifying and mitigating risks, driving down failure rates, and ultimately shortening the product validation lifecycle through smart automation and data-driven insights.
