Professional Experience

Featured Role: Product Yield Engineering Intern @ AMS OSRAM

In a high-volume semiconductor manufacturing environment, my primary mission was to enhance product yield and operational efficiency. I achieved this by leading initiatives to modernize legacy data systems, automate mission-critical analytics, and deploy real-time monitoring solutions that provided immediate, actionable insights to the engineering teams.

Data Infrastructure Modernization & Analytics Acceleration

The yield engineering team was bottlenecked by a slow, legacy on-premise manufacturing database, causing significant delays in analysis. I engineered and led the end-to-end migration to Snowflake Cloud. This involved architecting the new cloud schema, developing robust data validation processes, and systematically refactoring over 30 business-critical Power BI reports to connect to the new source.

Impact: Cut data refresh times by 40%, dramatically improving analytics velocity and enabling faster, data-driven decision-making for the entire yield team.

Scalable ETL Pipeline Automation

Manual ETL processes consumed dozens of engineering hours weekly and were prone to human error. I developed a suite of fully automated and scalable Databricks notebooks using PySpark to handle the ingestion, cleaning, and aggregation of terabytes of manufacturing data. The solution was designed for resilience and easy maintenance.

Impact: Slashed data processing times by 75% and liberated significant engineering hours, allowing the team to focus on higher-value analysis and proactive yield improvement.

Real-Time Equipment KPI Monitoring Dashboard

Operational decisions were hindered by a legacy Excel tool that took over 15 minutes to load equipment data. I replaced this obsolete tool by designing and deploying a high-performance Python web application using the Dash framework. The new dashboard provided sub-second access to critical equipment performance indicators.

Impact: Empowered floor supervisors and engineers to make immediate, data-driven operational adjustments, reducing equipment downtime and improving process control.

High-Throughput Defect Inspection Pipeline

The analysis of high-resolution images from CCD-based defect inspection systems was slow due to massive file sizes. I architected and implemented an intelligent image compression pipeline in Python/OpenCV that reduced data size without compromising the critical feature visibility required for accurate defect classification models.

Impact: Achieved a >90% reduction in image data size, dramatically accelerating the entire defect reporting and analysis workflow.

System Health & Uptime Monitoring

To ensure our new data tools were always available, I developed and deployed a proactive monitoring and alerting system. These automated scripts continuously checked the health of mission-critical engineering and reliability dashboards.

Impact: Guaranteed over 90% uptime for key analytics platforms, ensuring constant data availability for incident response and failure trend monitoring.
